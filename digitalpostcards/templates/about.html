Digital Postcards for Edinburgh Collected

Share memories from Edinburgh Collected

Digital Postcards is an Open Source Javascript Module and Webservice to share content with others in a personal manner.

The project is available on github: github .com/clementmouchet/digital-postcards under the MIT License (MIT).

Edinburgh Collected is a place to share, explore and discuss your memories of Edinburgh.
It is managed and maintained by Edinburgh Libraries, part of the City of Edinburgh Council (CEC).
To find out more about the project please visit: https://www.edinburghcollected.org/about
